Survey Results

As part of our development process, we surveyed students at GCU to understand their needs and concerns regarding vehicular security:

  • 85% of respondents expressed concern about car break-ins.
  • 90% stated they would consider purchasing a low-cost dashcam solution.
  • 70% were interested in AI-driven motion detection features.
  • 50% of respondents reported being victims of vehicular theft or vandalism.

These insights have driven the design and features of Trojan Eyes, ensuring we address the needs of the community.
